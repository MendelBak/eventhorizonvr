Event Horizon VR Services

Event Horizon VR is committed to providing a unique and unforgettable entertainment option for events of all shapes and sizes including Corporate Events, Birthday Parties, Bar/Bat Mitzvahs, Fundraisers, Holiday Parties, and more.

We provide a full service VR rental experience which includes all the required equipment and trained staff to run VR at your event, as well as custom selected games and experiences to fit all ages of partygoers as well as the theme of your party.

Each Virtual Reality setup includes a HTC Vive HMD (Head Mounted Display), two Vive wand controllers, two Lighthouse Basestations, and a large TV to display what is being experienced in VR for a fully interactive and social experience as well as other ancillary components.
